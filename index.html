What We Build

Services &
Capabilities

The building blocks of decentralized confidential cloud computing — hardware-secured, regulation-aware, and built for trustworthy agentic AI.

⬕

01

Hardware-Assured Security

Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs), confidential virtual machines (CVMs), and hardware-enforced sandboxing guarantee data confidentiality and code integrity at the silicon level. Each service runs in an isolated enclave — verifiably protected even across untrusted cloud nodes, third-party operators, and shared multi-tenant infrastructure. No trust assumptions required.

◧

02

Policy-Compliant by Design

A declarative policy language that bridges legal text and executable runtime constraints — encoding GDPR, EU AI Act, and organizational rules as machine-readable, human-auditable policies. Automated compliance enforcement is verifiably applied at the compute layer, closing the gap between regulation and technical execution.

◈

03

Secure Storage System

Encrypted-at-rest, access-controlled storage with fine-grained data governance. Integrity-verified, policy-bound storage that ensures sensitive data is only ever accessible under verified, attested conditions. 

⟳

04

Trustworthy AI Agent

Autonomous AI agents that are verifiably safe, policy-bound, and auditable by design. Running inside confidential enclaves, every agent action is hardware-attested, regulation-compliant, and constrained by declarative policies — guaranteeing trustworthy behaviour from planning to execution.

◫

05

Confidential Nested Virtualization

Lightweight nested VM isolation that preserves hardware-level confidentiality guarantees across virtualization layers. Run isolated confidential workloads inside existing cloud VMs — with minimal overhead and full TEE attestation at every nesting level.

◎

06

Provable Security

End-to-end security guarantees that go beyond declaration — mathematically and cryptographically provable. Remote attestation verifies the integrity of hardware, firmware, and software before any workload executes. Accountability mechanisms ensure every action is traceable to a verified identity. Full auditability means every computation, decision, and data access is logged, attested, and inspectable — across the entire decentralized stack.

Applications

Use Cases

Decentralized confidential cloud computing deployed across high-stakes, regulated industries where data privacy and AI trust are non-negotiable.

⚕

Trustworthy Intelligent Medical Services

Healthcare AI operates on the most sensitive data imaginable — patient records, genomic information, clinical histories. DTIS enables hospitals, research institutions, and health networks to deploy AI diagnostics, predictive models, and agentic clinical tools with hardware-guaranteed confidentiality and full GDPR compliance, even across multi-institutional collaborations.

Key Challenges Addressed

  • Patient data must never leave compliant environments, even during AI inference and model training.
  • Multi-hospital AI collaboration requires sharing insights without exposing raw clinical records.
  • Autonomous clinical AI agents must operate under strict, verifiable policy constraints.
  • EU AI Act classifies medical AI as high-risk — demanding auditability and explainability.

DTIS Solutions

Confidential AI Diagnostics

Run diagnostic models inside TEE-secured enclaves — patient data is never exposed to the cloud operator or other tenants.

Federated Clinical Learning

Train shared models across hospital networks using privacy-preserving federated learning, secured at each node by hardware attestation.

Policy-Bound Medical Agents

Autonomous AI agents constrained by declarative policies encoding GDPR, medical ethics, and institutional guidelines — verifiably enforced at runtime.

Compliant Audit Trails

Every AI decision is hardware-attested and logged — meeting EU AI Act high-risk requirements for transparency and auditability.

◈

Trustworthy Intelligent Financial Services

Financial institutions process vast volumes of proprietary and client data while navigating dense regulatory landscapes. DTIS enables banks, asset managers, and fintech operators to deploy agentic AI for fraud detection, risk modelling, and automated decision-making — with hardware-assured security, regulatory compliance, and zero trust in the underlying cloud infrastructure.

Key Challenges Addressed

  • Proprietary trading strategies and client portfolios cannot be exposed to cloud operators.
  • AI-driven financial decisions must be auditable for MiFID II, DORA, and GDPR regulators.
  • Multi-party risk models require cross-institution data collaboration without data sharing.
  • Autonomous trading and compliance agents must operate within strict, policy-enforced boundaries.

DTIS Solutions

Confidential Risk Modelling

Run proprietary risk and pricing models in TEE enclaves — outputs are verifiable while inputs remain cryptographically sealed.

Regulatory Audit Infrastructure

Hardware-attested decision logs satisfy MiFID II and DORA auditability requirements without exposing sensitive model internals.

Cross-Institution Fraud Detection

Collaborative fraud models trained across competing institutions using federated confidential learning — no raw transaction data ever shared.

Policy-Gated Autonomous Agents

Compliance and trading agents constrained by machine-readable regulatory policies, enforced in hardware — reducing operational and legal risk.

⚖

Trustworthy Intelligent Legal Services

Legal AI handles the most confidential documents in existence — privileged communications, M&A negotiations, litigation strategy, and client contracts. DTIS allows law firms and legal tech platforms to deploy powerful AI research, drafting, and agentic legal workflows under ironclad attorney-client privilege guarantees and full regulatory compliance, without trusting any cloud provider.

Key Challenges Addressed

  • Attorney-client privilege demands absolute confidentiality — cloud providers cannot be trusted with legal documents.
  • Cross-border legal work requires compliance with divergent data sovereignty and GDPR obligations.
  • Autonomous legal agents must operate within strict jurisdictional and ethical policy constraints.
  • AI-assisted legal decisions require explainability and auditability for professional liability.

DTIS Solutions

Privilege-Protected AI Research

Legal document analysis and case research in TEE enclaves — privileged content is hardware-sealed, never visible to the cloud operator.

Data Sovereignty Controls

Declarative policies enforce data residency and cross-border transfer restrictions — automatically and verifiably, per jurisdiction.

Policy-Compliant Legal Agents

Agentic AI for contract drafting and due diligence, constrained by firm-level and jurisdictional ethical policies — attested at every step.

Auditable AI Decisions

Every AI-assisted legal output is hardware-attested and logged, supporting professional accountability and malpractice risk management.
